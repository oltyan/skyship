Welcome to SkyShip, a tool designed to provision, centrally manage, and keep track of your cloud infrastructure. SkyShip can help you reduce overhead costs in managing your infrastructure, taking your most tedious tasks and providing an automation framework for you. With its flexibility and ability to leverage all the major cloud service providers, you can deploy and manage infrastructure locally to globally using a single interface. SkyShip has a central online management console that you can use or you can contact one of our partners to purchase your own internal private cloud that leverages our technology.
